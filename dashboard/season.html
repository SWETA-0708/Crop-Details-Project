Kharif Season

Kharif crops are sown with the onset of the monsoon in June and harvested in September-October. These crops require a lot of water and hot weather to grow.

Crops Grown

  • Rice
  • Cotton
  • Soybean
  • Sugarcane
  • Maize

Rabi Season

Rabi crops are sown in winter from October to December and harvested in summer from April to June. These crops require a cooler climate to grow and ripen.

Crops Grown

  • Wheat
  • Barley
  • Mustard
  • Peas
  • Chickpea

Zaid Season

Zaid crops are grown between the Kharif and Rabi seasons, mainly from March to June. These crops are typically fast-growing and require warm, dry weather.

Crops Grown

  • Cucumber
  • Watermelon
  • Muskmelon
  • Pumpkin
  • Moong Dal
